Hobbies

Kung-fu

Kung-Fu (功夫) translates to "skill" or "achievement" through hard work and practice to hone one's spirit, mind and body as unified whole. Kung-fu is never-ending cycle of self-improvement and pushing oneself to the limits to be better than yesterday. To this day, I have been practicing Kung-fu for 5 years and I am still learning new things every day.

Surfing

Surfing is a thrilling and challenging hobby that requires both physical and mental strength. The feeling of riding a wave and being at one with the ocean is indescribable. You just have to try it to understand. It is a great way to put your mind out of the daily stress and enjoy the beauty of ocean. You can also catch a tan which is nice bonus.

Qigong

Qigong is a practice that involves controlled breathing, movements, and meditation. It goes hand in hand with kung-fu and has become an essential part of my daily routine. By focusing on my breath and clearing my mind, I am able to find a sense of peace and tranquility within myself. Through regular practice, I have noticed improvements in my physical health and mental well-being.
